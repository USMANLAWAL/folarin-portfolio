About Me

Creative Designer & Frontend Developer with 4 Years of Experience in UI/UX, Content Creation, and Digital Branding.

I'm a dedicated and versatile designer with a strong foundation in UI/UX design, frontend development, and content creation. Over the past 4 years, I've honed my skills in crafting responsive layouts, building brand visuals, and creating dynamic content for social media and websites.

UI/UX Design: From wireframes to polished prototypes, I bring user-centered designs to life, ensuring an intuitive experience across all devices.

Frontend Development: With proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I build responsive websites and landing pages that prioritize both functionality and aesthetics.

Content Creation & Social Media Management: I create engaging social media content that boosts brand visibility and audience engagement. My background includes managing posts, designing promotional materials, and developing video content to enhance brand awareness.

Brand Design & Visual Identity: From designing logos to creating cohesive brand visuals, I’m passionate about building identities that resonate with audiences.

With a problem-solving mindset, strong organizational skills, and experience in IT support, I’m committed to delivering high-quality designs that meet client goals. Whether collaborating with teams or working independently, I strive to make each project impactful and memorable.
